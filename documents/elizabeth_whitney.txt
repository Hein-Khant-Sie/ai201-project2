Professor: Elizabeth Whitney

eview 1:
Students frequently describe Professor Whitney as caring, accessible, and responsive to emails. Many reviews mention that she helps students when they have questions and provides support throughout the course.

Several students report that her classes are straightforward and relatively easy if assignments are completed on time and instructions are followed carefully.

Review 3:
Many reviews mention weekly discussion boards, reading assignments, and peer responses as regular course requirements.

Review 4:
