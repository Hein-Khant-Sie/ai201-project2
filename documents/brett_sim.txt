Professor: Brett Sim
Source: https://www.ratemyprofessors.com/professor/1558787

Review 1:
Students frequently describe Professor Sims as friendly, respectful, and approachable. Many mention that he is willing to help students outside class.

Review 2:
Several reviews highlight his sense of humor and say that his personality makes classes more enjoyable and engaging.

Review 3:
Students often report that completing homework regularly is the key to success in his courses because exam questions are closely related to assigned practice problems.

Review 4:
Many reviews mention opportunities for extra credit through participation and class engagement.

Review 5:
Students generally describe his lectures as clear and effective, especially for difficult mathematics topics such as calculus.

Review 6:
Several students say that he explains concepts thoroughly and spends time reviewing homework problems during class.

Review 7:
Some reviews note that lectures can occasionally be difficult to follow, but students who practice independently and use additional resources are able to succeed.

Review 8:
Many students report that he provides feedback, answers questions, and is accessible outside of class.

Review 9:
Several reviews describe the workload as substantial, with regular homework assignments that require consistent effort.

Review 10:
Some students praise his grading fairness and flexibility, while a small number of reviews criticize grading policies or exam-heavy assessment methods.

Review 11:
Students often describe him as inspirational, supportive, and one of the strongest math professors at BMCC.

Review 12:
Overall reviews suggest that students who participate, complete homework, and stay engaged in class usually perform well and would recommend taking his courses.
