Professor: Louise Yan
Source: https://www.ratemyprofessors.com/professor/2262999

Review 1:
Students describe Professor Yan as knowledgeable and strong at explaining programming concepts, especially C++ and computer science fundamentals.

Review 2:
Several students say she codes live during lectures instead of only reading from slides. Students who like live coding examples find this helpful for understanding how programs are built.

Review 3:
Many positive reviews say she explains topics clearly, makes sure students understand concepts, and is willing to answer repeated questions.

Review 4:
Some students describe her as organized, clear, kind, and one of their favorite professors at BMCC.

Review 5:
Several students say her courses are fast paced and require students to pay close attention during class.

Review 6:
Many reviews mention that exams and quizzes are important parts of the grade. Some students describe the class as test-heavy, with quizzes, a midterm, and a final.

Review 7:
Some students warn that her exams can be difficult, especially because students may need to write full programs or code on paper.

Review 8:
Several negative reviews say the lecture pace is too fast and that students who fall behind may struggle.

Review 9:
Students describe mixed experiences with grading. Some say grading is fair and clear, while others mention tough grading or late grading.

Review 10:
Some reviews mention limited flexibility, such as no retakes, no extra credit, or heavy weight on major exams.

Review 11:
Positive reviews say she genuinely cares about students and wants them to learn the material rather than just pass.

Review 12:
Overall, reviews suggest Professor Yan is a strong computer science instructor for students who are prepared to keep up, attend class, study consistently, and actively practice programming.
