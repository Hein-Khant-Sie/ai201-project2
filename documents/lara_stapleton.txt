Professor: Lara Stapleton
Source: https://www.ratemyprofessors.com/professor/376128

Review 1:
Students consistently describe Professor Stapleton as caring, supportive, and dedicated to student success. Many reviews mention that she creates a welcoming classroom environment where students feel comfortable learning and participating.

Review 2:
A common theme across reviews is her detailed and constructive feedback on essays and writing assignments. Students report that her comments help them improve their writing skills throughout the semester.

Review 3:
Many students praise her ability to teach writing fundamentals, including essay structure, source citation, annotation, and analytical writing. Reviews frequently state that they became stronger writers after taking her class.

Review 4:
Students often describe Professor Stapleton as passionate about teaching English and genuinely invested in helping students learn. Her enthusiasm is frequently mentioned in reviews.

Review 5:
Several reviews note that attendance, participation, and arriving on time are important expectations in her classes. Students are encouraged to actively engage in discussions and classroom activities.

Review 6:
Many reviews mention a moderate amount of reading, essays, and homework. While the workload can be substantial at times, students generally describe it as manageable when assignments are completed consistently.

Review 7:
Students frequently praise her fair grading practices and clear expectations. Reviews often mention that grading criteria are transparent and that feedback is provided in a timely manner.

Review 8:
Several students describe Professor Stapleton as approachable, understanding, and willing to help outside of class. Students report that she responds to questions and provides support when needed.

Review 9:
A recurring theme in reviews is that she motivates students who previously disliked English or writing. Many students state that her teaching increased their confidence as writers.

Review 10:
Students frequently describe her lectures and class discussions as engaging, relevant, and connected to real-life experiences. Readings are often viewed as meaningful and thought-provoking.

Review 11:
Some reviews mention occasional pop quizzes, regular reading assignments, and essay deadlines, requiring students to stay organized throughout the semester.

Review 12:
Overall, students describe Professor Stapleton as one of the strongest English instructors at BMCC, highlighting her feedback, fairness, enthusiasm, and commitment to student growth.
