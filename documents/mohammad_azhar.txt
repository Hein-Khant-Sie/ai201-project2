Review 1:
Students frequently describe Professor Azhar as caring, supportive, and invested in student success both inside and outside the classroom.

Review 3:

Review 4:

Review 5:

Review 6:

Review 7:
Reviews are mixed regarding teaching style. Some students find his explanations helpful and effective, while others feel the course relies heavily on self-study and independent learning.

Review 8:

Review 9:
Students consistently note that his honors courses are challenging but rewarding. Several reviews mention that the difficulty prepares students for internships, four-year colleges, and professional software development environments.

Review 10:
Professor Azhar is often described as accessible outside class and willing to answer questions when students need additional support.

Review 11:
Some negative reviews state that the pace can be difficult to follow and that students who are not confident in coding may struggle with the workload and expectations.

Review 12:
Overall, reviews suggest Professor Azhar is best suited for motivated students who want strong preparation, professional opportunities, and substantial coding experience, even if it requires significant effort and independent learning.
