Professor: Kenneth Levinson
Source: https://www.ratemyprofessors.com/professor/1499892

Review 1:
Students frequently describe Professor Levinson as knowledgeable, intelligent, and passionate about language, linguistics, and writing. Many reviews mention that his lectures are clear, informative, and engaging.

Review 2:
A common theme across reviews is the heavy reading and writing workload. Students often report weekly essays, discussion posts, lengthy reading assignments, and writing-intensive coursework.

Review 3:
Several students note that Professor Levinson provides detailed feedback on assignments and is willing to help students improve their writing and critical thinking skills.

Review 4:
Many reviews describe him as caring, understanding, and flexible. Students mention that he often allows late work and works with students who fall behind.

Review 5:
Students frequently mention that success in his courses requires strong time management. Reviews warn against procrastination because assignments can accumulate quickly.

Review 6:
Several reviews praise his use of Blackboard and online course materials. Students report that instructions, readings, assignments, and course resources are organized and accessible.

Review 7:
Many students state that exams are fair and manageable if readings and assignments are completed consistently throughout the semester.

Review 8:
Some reviews describe Professor Levinson as intimidating at first, but most students report that he becomes approachable and supportive once they get to know him.

Review 9:
Students often mention that his classes encourage critical thinking, discussion, and active engagement with course materials rather than simple memorization.

Review 10:
Several reviews note that linguistics courses can be challenging due to the amount of reading, writing, and discussion required, but students generally feel they learn a great deal.

Review 11:
A small number of reviews criticize the workload or mention that grades can sometimes feel difficult to understand, especially when detailed grading explanations are not provided.

Review 12:
Overall, students describe Professor Levinson as a caring and effective instructor who challenges students academically while providing substantial support and feedback throughout the course.
