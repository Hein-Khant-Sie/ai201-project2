Professor: Jenna Hirsch
Source: https://www.ratemyprofessors.com/professor/804692

Review 1:
Students consistently describe Professor Hirsch as passionate about mathematics and deeply committed to student success. Many reviews mention that she genuinely wants students to understand the material and succeed in class.

Review 2:
Several students praise her ability to explain difficult mathematical concepts in a clear and intuitive way. Reviews often mention that complex topics become easier to understand through her teaching style.

Review 3:
Students frequently describe her lectures as engaging, energetic, and informative. Many reviews highlight her enthusiasm and positive classroom atmosphere.

Review 4:
A common theme in reviews is that Professor Hirsch is caring, approachable, and responsive to student questions. Students report receiving quick email responses and additional help when needed.

Review 5:
Many reviews mention that attendance and participation are important components of the course. Students are encouraged to stay engaged and ask questions during class.

Review 6:
Several students note that the workload can be heavy, including homework assignments, quizzes, and regular practice. However, most reviews state that the workload helps reinforce learning.

Review 7:
Students often mention that exams can be challenging and that the pace of the course may feel fast at times, especially in higher-level mathematics courses.

Review 8:
Reviews frequently describe Professor Hirsch as patient and willing to explain concepts multiple times until students understand the material.

Review 9:
Some students praise her use of real-life examples, analogies, humor, and creative teaching techniques to make mathematics more approachable.

Review 10:
Several reviews mention study sessions, review opportunities, and extra support before major exams and quizzes.

Review 11:
A small number of reviews mention that the course can be difficult for students who struggle with mathematics or fall behind on assignments.

Review 12:
Overall, students describe Professor Hirsch as one of the strongest mathematics instructors at BMCC, praising her teaching ability, enthusiasm, accessibility, and dedication to student learning.
