Review 1:
Some students reported that points were deducted from assignments without explanation and that feedback was limited.
Review 2:
Many students described ECO 201 as relatively easy. Weekly work often consisted of one discussion post, one response to classmates, and one assignment.
Review 3:
Several reviews emphasized that submitting work on time is important because assignments may close after the due date.
Review 4:
Students in asynchronous sections reported there were no exams and that grading criteria were clear.
Review 5:
Many students stated that reading the textbook and PowerPoint materials was necessary for success in the course.
Review 6:
Some students described Professor Duncan as caring, understanding, and willing to help students facing personal challenges.
Review 7:
Students frequently mentioned that attendance and participation mattered in in-person sections.
Review 8:

Review 9:
Many students said the course was easy to pass if assignments were completed consistently and on time.
Review 10:
